Lemon and Parsley Cauliflower

Description

Fresh lemon and parsley make eating cauliflower simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 2 TBSP olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • garlic powder
  • red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley
  • 1 large lemon

Directions

  1. Chop the cauliflower into bite-sized pieces and place on aluminum baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil and add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and crushed red pepper flakes. Roast in the oven at 450 degrees Farenheit for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove from oven and place in bowl. Add juice from 1-2 lemons and chopped parsley. Enjoy.
